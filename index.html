Our Story

Sip N Chill was born from a vision: to transform everyday convenience into a luxury experience. While vending machines are common in the USA, in Kerala we’re introducing the very first *premium branded vending machines* — strategically placed at hotspots, streets, gyms, and more. We believe grabbing a Coke or a protein drink should feel just as stylish as sipping coffee at a high-end café.
