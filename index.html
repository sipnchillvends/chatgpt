Our Story

Sip N Chill was born from a simple yet powerful idea — to bring the luxury of global vending technology to Kerala for the very first time. In countries like the USA, premium vending machines are an everyday convenience. Here, we saw an opportunity to create something revolutionary: fully branded, beautifully designed machines serving quality drinks, fitness supplements, and desserts in prime locations across the state. We are redefining convenience, one Sip N Chill moment at a time.
